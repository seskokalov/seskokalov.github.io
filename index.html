Our Story
We named it PhiloStack because we believe great software is built by people who genuinely love the craft — and understand the business behind it.

Based in North Macedonia with clients across Europe, PhiloStack was born from years of building and maintaining the systems that retail businesses depend on every day — from ERP automations and POS integrations to real-time analytics dashboards.

We're not a factory. We're a focused team that goes deep on every project, because we've learned that the details in a stored procedure or a data model are the difference between software that works and software that transforms.
